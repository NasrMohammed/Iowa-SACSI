Sudanese Community Center Mission

  • Foster Community of education, informed and engaged with the City of Iowa City
  • The assist of our Community to strengthen democracy for all new Sudanese families and children to have the opportunity that they need
  • To involve all the Community in sports for health
  • To build a bridge between our old members and the newcomers
  • To preserve the Sudanese cultures and traditions among the communities
  • To collaborate with the reset of the communities in the City of Iowa City
  • To empower our local community to be self sufficient in order to be financially independent
